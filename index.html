Spectre

Go from a boring hex color to an exciting named one!

About

“Great, just what we need, another novelty single page web app.”

The CSS color spec has a bunch of named color keywords, ranging from the boring red and blue to the exotic goldenrod and moccasin. This helps you find the closest one to a hex/RGB color.

This was thrown together one Sunday by @adamaveray. It's very much not perfect.

You bet it’s on Github.
